Serverless Food Ordering Privacy Policy

NOTE THAT THIS IS A DEMO APPLICATION AND DOES NOT ACTUALLY CHARGE A CARD OR DELIVER FOOD.

What information do you collect?

This application collects various information when you interact with it. This information includes automatically collected usage information such as HTTP request logs, Dialogflow API functionality, and user identifiers, including your Google ID. If you choose to place an order, you will be prompted for transaction details. This information will be held by Google using Google Pay, and will only be transmitted in tokenized format to the Square testing API.

How do you use the information?

This information is used only for demo purposes. In the demo, you are able to place orders and check the status of your order. This should never send email or contact you outside the demo environment.

What information do you share?

Submitted credit card information for a transaction is handled via Google Pay and the Square TESTING infrastructure, and no cards should be charged. Information is also shared with Google through their Dialogflow and Actions on Google service, but your information will not be shared with other companies except as outlined above.
